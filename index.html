Ghost Run

An endless 2D side-scroller built with the Unity game engine using C#. The player takes control of a ghost and must traverse a world of randomly spawned enemies and platforms. The player must manage the use of their limited intagibility to either pass through platforms or to fight enemies.

  • For this project, I recruited a team of artists and programmers then led the team in the creation of a 2D game using the Unity game engine.
  • As project manager, I assessed the larger task and designed smaller goals for my team to follow.
  • As the most experienced with the engine, I directed my team on the software.
  • As the lead programmer, implemented the following features:
    • Collision detection (Player-enemy, ground/platform)
    • Player Life/death mechanic
    • Enemy AI
    • Randomized enemy and platform spawn logic
    • Background paralax scrolling
